‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to master DSA?

Typically 3-6 months of consistent study (1-2 hours daily) to have good fundamentals. Mastery takes years of practice.

Should I memorize algorithms?

No, focus on understanding the concepts. Recognition patterns help, but understanding is more important.

Is DSA necessary for every job?

Yes, for technical interviews at most tech companies. It's fundamental to computer science.

How do I practice effectively?

Write code daily, solve problems, review solutions, and implement from scratch without looking at solutions.
